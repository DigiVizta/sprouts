About Us

At Sprouts Vine Bistro, we believe that food is best enjoyed when shared. Our family-friendly restaurant combines warm hospitality with delicious flavors from our kitchen and bar. Whether you’re here for a casual dinner, a refreshing drink at the bar, or a grand celebration in our banquet hall, we make every visit special.

  • Freshly prepared meals with locally sourced ingredients.
  • A welcoming bar with classic and signature cocktails.
  • A spacious banquet hall perfect for birthdays, weddings, and corporate events.

Our chefs prepare dishes using fresh, locally sourced ingredients, paired with a handpicked wine selection. Whether you’re here for a quiet dinner, a celebration, or a casual get-together, we make every moment memorable.
